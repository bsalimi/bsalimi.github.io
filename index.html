Biography

I am a research associate at University of Washington and part of UW Database Group, where I work with Prof. Dan Suciu. I completed my Ph.D. in 2015 at  Carleton University, advised by Prof. Leopoldo Bertossi. I am a recipient of SIGMOD 2019 Best Paper Award and VLDB 2018 Best Demonstration Paper Award.

My research interests cover Data Management, Causal Inference, Decision Support Systems, Responsible Data Science and Data Ethics.

I am currently on the job market. Please see my research and teaching statements and curriculum vitae here.
